About Me

I'm a Data Analytics Manager focusing on datamart design, end-to-end analytics, dashboard systems, and AI applications to optimize business decision-making. I lead a talented team of analysts and build scalable data solutions.

“In God we trust. All others must bring data.” – W. Edwards Deming

I’m an INTJ — the personality type known for loving systems, optimizing everything, and constantly asking “why.” From early on, I was drawn to anything that allowed me to see the world through a lens of logic, structure, and measurable patterns.

At some point, I realized that data was the perfect match for my mindset — a domain where economics, technology, and analytical thinking intersect.

I pursued research seriously, diving into complex topics and running models that kept me up through the night. Those efforts led to multiple national-level research awards, but more importantly, they gave me a core skill: reading data to uncover the truth behind a problem.

Today, my work centers on turning data into real, measurable value:

  • Understanding what customers truly need
  • Optimizing operations
  • Supporting strategic decision-making
  • Building scalable systems for long-term growth
“The goal is to turn data into information, and information into insight.” – Carly Fiorina

That’s what I strive for every day — transforming data into insight, and insight into impact.

If you also believe in the power of numbers to tell meaningful stories, I’d be glad to connect with you.
